Our mission

Modino-io is a platform for IoT vulnerability management and over-the-air update orchestration at scale. A platform that is both safe and secure. Our mission is to keep our clients' IoT devices secure and online by providing stressless over-the-air update experience. Błażej Pawlak, Co-Founder

/moˈdiːno/

“Modino” is an Italian word used for misbehaving children, which roughly translates to “behave yourself”. We imagine our system as a guardian for various small IoT devices that keeps them in check and makes sure that they all behave as they should.
